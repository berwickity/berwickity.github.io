About

Bernice Wicks likes wrens, and the way they collectively dive into a bush. She also likes the sudden appearance of foxgloves on a bike ride, and the ocean on a windy June night.

She is a settler located on the lands of the Lands of the Xwsepsum (Esquimalt) and Lekwungen (Songhees) ancestors and families, and recognizes that settler oppression of Indigenous peoples exists and a more equitable society must be created.

She has been lucky to be published by Geist Magazine.
